Hi there! I’m a postdoc working with Dr. Parker MacCready. I’m interested in understanding how estuarine and coastal circulations influence biological or biogeochemical processes, such as harmful algal bloom, hypoxia, and eDNA. To answer “What does physics 🌊 do to biology🐟”, I use realistic oceanographic numerical models, including LiveOcean (built based on ROMS), SCHISM, and EFDC. I have experience in working on two large estuarine systems, the Chesapeake Bay and the Salish Sea. I got my Ph.D. from Virginia Institute of Marine Science, advised by Dr. Jian Shen.

Outside of research, I enjoy hiking, jogging, and road trips.
